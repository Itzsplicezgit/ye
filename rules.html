Tournament Rules

General

  • All players must be officially registered before the tournament begins.
  • Teams consist of five players on the field.
  • Matches will start at the scheduled time. Late teams may forfeit.
  • Cleats NOT ALLOWED!

Gameplay

  • Standard soccer rules apply unless stated otherwise.
  • Unsportsmanlike behavior will not be tolerated.
  • Referee decisions are final.
  • Goalies are optional.
  • If a player is more than halfway in front of the last defender they are offside.

Substitutions

  • Unlimited substitutions are allowed during stoppages.
  • Substitutions must be approved by the referee.

Discipline

  • Yellow cards act as warnings.
  • Two yellow cards equal a red card.
  • Red cards result in immediate removal from the match.

Advancement

  • Ties may be decided by penalty shootouts.

Final Notes

  • Organizers reserve the right to modify rules at anytime if necessary.
  • Respect players, referees, and staff at all times.
